About

I am a Paris based interactive art director, curently work at Octave & Octave. I create visual identities and interactive design systems for cultural, commercial and editorial clients.

I combine graphic design craftsmanship with interactive best practices. My understanding of technology helps me design for high performance websites. I have solid experience in user-centered websites and apps, never losing sight of the client’s business requirements.

Since 2011, I've been working on interactive stuff for many clients such as Orange Business services, LVMH Group, Arianespace, GDF SUEZ, Vinci Airports, PSA Group, L'Oréal, Unibail - Rodamco, Etam...

For any collaboration please drop me a line.
